Chill Places

Atlantic Place

(215 Water Street)

Nestled downtown, Atlantic Place is a brightly lit area with lots of seating. It also contains a Jumping Bean cafe and a gym.

Bannerman Brewery

(90 Duckworth Street)

A nice downtown cafe that transitions into a bar at night. It’s very busy on afternoons and evenings, especially on Fridays.

Jumping Bean

(37 Elizabeth Avenue)

Jumping Bean on Elizabeth Avenue is where CTS folks meetup on Thursdays, it’s a bit busy at times but has a great vibe.

Jumping Bean

(115 Kelsey Drive)

This is an underrated spot that lots of nearby workers go to.

Memorial University QEII Library

(234 Elizabeth Ave)

A lovely place for students to borrow books, grab a snack, study, and work remotely.

Rocket Bakery

(46C Bannister Street, Mount Pearl)

This bakery is a good spot to get some work done. It has nice coffee and food, without many people around most times.

The Avalon Mall

(48 Kenmount Road)

A great place for shopping, not a great place to work from. It has outlets and wifi but is very busy and loud.

The Battery Cafe

(1 Duckworth Street)

This cafe is a small cozy spot near Signal Hill with limited seating. It's not too loud and has some great snacks.

The Cove

(21 St. Michaels Avenue)

The Cove is a lovely cafe with a rock climbing gym attached. It’s a wonderful place to sit down and code.

Starbucks

(191 Kenmount Road)

Starbucks is a safe bet, with lot of outlets and steady wi-fi available. They have some fun colorful drinks and great cake pops too.

Tim Hortons

(275 Kenmount Road)

You can find an outlet or two at this Tim Hortons, but unfortunately the Wi-Fi doesn’t work well.
